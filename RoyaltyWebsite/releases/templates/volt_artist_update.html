Where to find the Apple Music ID

The Apple Music ID is a unique string of characters that identifies an artist profile on Apple Music.

1. Go to the artist page on Apple Music and click on the 3 dots below the profile picture.

2. Select Share from the menu and click Copy link.

3. The Apple Music ID is the string of numbers located at the end, as highlighted below.

Where to find the Spotify ID

The Spotify ID is a unique string of characters that identifies an artist profile on Spotify.

1. Go to the artist page on Spotify and click on the 3 dots below the artist name.

2. Select Share from the menu and click Copy link to artist.

3. The Spotify ID is the string of characters located between /artist/ and ?, as highlighted below.
